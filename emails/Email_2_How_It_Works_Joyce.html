How AniMeals keeps Joyce's whole household fed and together.

Joyce's house is never quiet. There's Daniel, her dog, and two cats who have claimed every sunny windowsill in the place.

For a senior on a fixed income, feeding three animals is no small thing. Giving any of them up was never something Joyce wanted to think about, and through AniMeals she doesn't have to.

Here's how it works. The same volunteers who deliver Joyce her Meals on Wheels carry food for Daniel and the cats right to her door, on the same trip. It's a program a member of our team named Charity built from the ground up, one client at a time, because she kept meeting seniors who were sharing their own dinners with the pets they love.

Today AniMeals does that for whole households, so Joyce stays fed, her pets stay fed, and they all stay together.
