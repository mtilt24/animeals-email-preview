Friend of Seniors First,

For 25 years, Shirley was a chaplain. She spent her career showing up for people on their hardest days. These days, the one who shows up for her is Candy Kisses, her 11-year-old poodle.

Candy Kisses has been beside her through the loss of her husband and a move to a new community. When lifting heavy bags of dog food became too much, AniMeals began delivering it to Shirley's door along with her own meals.

"Seniors First and AniMeals have been a true blessing, not only for me but also for my beloved Candy Kisses."

Shirley, Placer County

Shirley is one of many. In a single year, AniMeals has enrolled 124 Meals on Wheels neighbors and now feeds 65 cats and 36 dogs across Placer County, more than 18,000 pounds of pet food carried to the door.
