Amber is a 13-year-old Goldendoodle, and she is the reason Janise keeps a routine.

When weakness made daily life harder this year, Janise joined Meals on Wheels. For a senior who lives quietly, a pet is not just a pet. It's a reason to get up, a warm presence in the house, and someone to care for when the days feel long.

Through AniMeals, Janise gets her own meals and Amber gets her food, so Janise never has to choose between her own nutrition and her dog's. They get to stay exactly where they belong, together.

That's what your gift protects, the bond that keeps a senior connected and comforted. We're raising $15,000 by July 15 to keep AniMeals going.
